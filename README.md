### Assorted OpenModelica  models for NEP (work in progress), built for export to FMU (co-simulation):
- Point kinetics with decay heat precursor tracking and finite power history
- Lumped conductive heat pipe model including vapour flow 'activation' to capture start-up behaviour and empirical throughput limits
- 1D radial core conduction model with simple radiative heat loss
- Lumped-conductive and mechanical-thermodynamic Stirling engine models (Sunpower ASC-E2 (KRUSTY), Sunpower RE-1000)
